Final Project

This was the final project in my bootcamp. We spent countless hours outside of class working on this app. CareConnect is a dedicated community-based application designed to bridge the gap between those who need assistance and those willing to offer a helping hand within their local community. Serving the sick, elderly, disabled, moms, and more, this platform empowers users to request specific help, whether it's a ride to a doctor's appointment, a meal, or tech assistance. By integrating shared calendar and posts features, CareConnect enhances the connection between community members. Allowing users to plan, volunteer, and coordinate assistance seamlessly.
